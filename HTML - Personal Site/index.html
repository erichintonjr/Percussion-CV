My name is Eric Hinton Jr and I am a percussionist that specializes in battery percussion.

Playing Experience


My experience includes 4 years with the Pflugerville High School Band (first year on Auxillary, next three years on snare.) Along with leadership experience there, I also won multiple solo contests for scholarship. Beyond high school, I spent a season with UTSA Indoor Percussion in 2019, a PIO group where we placed 7th in finals (92.763). Most recently I marched snare for the Blue Knights DBC, a World Class Corps where we placed 7th in finals (92.05)

Teaching Experience


I have been teaching as a drumline technician at Pflugerville High school for the 2018 and 2019 marching seasons, where I plan to return for 2020 and soforth.
